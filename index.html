Privacy Policy for VideoVroom

Last updated: November 11, 2025

Overview

VideoVroom is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

Data Collection

VideoVroom does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are tracked
  • No data is sent to third parties
  • All video processing happens locally on your device
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No account creation or login is required

Permissions

VideoVroom requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch videos from URLs you provide

These permissions are used solely for the app's functionality and no data is collected through them.

Third-Party Services

VideoVroom does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

VideoVroom does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
